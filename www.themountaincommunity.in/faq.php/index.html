Shipping

The Mountain Community currently ships to all over the country (INDIA), but not internationally.
We are working closely with our external partners to ensure yourpackage can reach youas quickly as possible. Once your order is placed successfully and we processed it you will get your package tracking details to your registered email address.

Here’s how it work: You order -  We process –Then we ship

  • Ordering takes a few seconds.
  • Processing takes 12hours to 24hours. We will ship orders placed before 1pm on the same day. All orders placed after 1pm will be shipped the following business day. Then our delivery partners takes it from there.
  • You’ll get a shipment notification email to your registered email address with order tracking details as soon as your package ships. It’s on the way to reach you.
Sorry about that! In a few cases, the package takes 1-2 business days to register your shipment status. If your tracking number isn’t updating or working, please contact us here as soon as you can, we can give you the inside info.
Delivery charges are additional to the price of the merchandise, and are calculated automatically once you have specified your total online order, prior to checkout. As we endeavor to keep costs reasonable for our customers, we do not charge any additional handling fees.
The safety of our team and customers is our top priority. We’re monitoring the situation very closely and working with our external fulfillment partners who are committed to taking all necessary safety precautions.

Wholesale

The Mountain Community delivers daily to cafes and offices across India. We work closely with these businesses to tailor our product and services to their specific needs. We look forward to working with you and invite you to contact us here.
We are pleased to offer you our best wholesale prices, for regular monthly orders of 20kg and above, without any contractual obligations. In return we ask all invoices be paid prior to dispatch by EFT or credit card.
We pride ourselves on keeping prices reasonable for all our customers and offers wholesale customers volume discounts. Please contact us here to discuss these options.
We batch roast each order fresh, and pack and dispatch within 48 business hours of receipt of your order. Our promise of prompt dispatch and delivery, means you will be offering the freshest coffee possible.
We are also happy to work with you to design your own unique customized blends, based on any of our coffees, including all our 100% single origin Arabica.
