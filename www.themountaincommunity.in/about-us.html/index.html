Our Story

Since we started, the aroma and taste of The Mountain Community's hand roasted coffee beans has filled all over country, and welcomed by our customers. We’re become an iconic destination for serious coffee and tea drinkers, and dedicated home baristas.

In collaboration with store, every day, we hand blend and carefully roast, a selection of specialty beans from leading growers. We offer single origin, blended and green bean coffees, and loose-leaf teas and infusions, through our brand label.

We are a family run business, dedicated to offering our customers speedy shipping, personalized customer service and expert advice. We believe that everyone should be able to enjoy the freshest coffee and teas, everyday.

Our Specialty Produce

We select only the finest quality specialty green beans and loose leaf teas, from select growing regions across the country, including; plantation, co-operative, organic, ethical and eco-friendly crops. Our specialty coffees and teas have been through a meticulous selection process, and represent the top grade produce available each season.

Our Single Origins and Unique Blends

Our superb single origin coffees form the base of many of our blends, and are exceptional tasting coffees in their own right. Each one is precisely hand roasted, to unlock the best characteristics of each unique region, estate or farm.

Coffee blending is a craft based on finding the right balance and proportion, for optimum flavor. Our roasters carefully blend various coffee varietals and origins, to create full character flavors. Coffee is a passion for us that is always evolving and changing.

Our Best Price Promise

We aim to keep our prices lower than our competitors, so you can afford to enjoy the best specialty coffees and tea everyday. How do we do this but still offer the highest produce? We directly manufacture our own coffees - so there are no extra costs or margins added to our prices.
